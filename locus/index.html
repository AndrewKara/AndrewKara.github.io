Locus

Focus Timer

A focus timer that makes deep work feel rewarding. Start a session, stay locked in, see your focused hours stack up over time. No gamification gimmicks — just clean, satisfying focus tracking.

Privacy

Locus stores all your focus session data locally on your device. We do not require accounts and your data never leaves your phone.

We collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g. sessions completed, app opens) via PostHog to improve the app. This data is not linked to your identity and contains no personal information. No advertising trackers are used.

Privacy

Resurface stores all your saved links and tags locally on your device. We do not require accounts and your data never leaves your phone.

We collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g. features used, app opens) via PostHog to improve the app. This data is not linked to your identity and contains no personal information. No advertising trackers are used.

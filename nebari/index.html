Privacy

Nebari stores all your tree and care data locally on your device in a SQLite database. We do not require accounts and your collection data never leaves your phone unless you choose to back it up.

Optional iCloud Drive backup writes a JSON file to your own iCloud Drive, which only you can access. We never see this file.

We collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g. which features are used, app opens) via PostHog to improve the app. This data is not linked to your identity and contains no personal information. No advertising trackers are used.

Reptile Tracker

Track your reptiles' feeding, shedding, weight, and enclosure conditions. The husbandry app that doesn't crash, doesn't charge monthly, and works offline.

Privacy

Shed stores all your animal data locally on your device. We do not require accounts and your collection data never leaves your phone.

We collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g. which features are used, app opens) via PostHog to improve the app. This data is not linked to your identity and contains no personal information. No advertising trackers are used.
